# Bridge Brigade – Rapid Deployment Emergency Shelter

## Overview

Bridge Brigade is a CAD-based emergency shelter prototype designed during CyberForge 2025. The project addresses the challenge of rapidly deployable shelters for disaster relief by combining portability, durability, sustainability, and ease of assembly.

Designed by a two-member team using SolidWorks.

---

## Problem Statement

Natural disasters such as earthquakes, floods, and hurricanes leave thousands homeless. The objective was to design an emergency shelter that could be transported easily, assembled quickly, withstand harsh weather, and be reusable.

---

## Solution

The proposed shelter uses an interlocking steel beam framework with lightweight aluminium panels to achieve rapid deployment while maintaining structural integrity and weather resistance.
